# Account Recovery Guide

If you have paid but cannot access your account, follow the instructions below depending on your situation.

## ✅ Case 1 — You subscribed earlier than today and can't see your past conversations

**What's happening:**
You are using a different email than the one linked to your subscription.

**Symptoms:**
- You see the subscription page instead of your past conversations
- Your conversations are missing
- In **Settings** (https://mammouth.ai/app/account/settings), the displayed email is, in fact, not the one expected

**Important note (Gmail users):**
Addresses like `john.campbell@gmail.com` and `johncampbell@gmail.com` are considered **different accounts** on Mammouth.

**How to recover your account:**
1. Log out (or delete the current account if needed)
2. Try logging in with your different email addresses
3. Repeat until you find the account where your conversations are visible

::: tip
Your invoice email can help, but it is **not always the same** as your account email. Even if you tried it, test other emails as well.
:::

## 💳 Case 2 — You subscribed less than 1 hour ago

**Most likely:** the payment failed.

::: warning
Some banking apps may temporarily show the payment as successful before marking it as failed.
:::

**How to check:**
- If you did **not receive an invoice email** (check spam), the payment failed

**What to do:**
- Wait until the pending transaction disappears from your bank account
- Try again (possibly with another payment method)

---

**If you received an invoice:**
- ✅ Payment was successful
- ⚠️ In rare cases, account activation can be delayed

**What to do:**
- Wait at least **1 hour** (automatic retries happen at 30 min and 1 hour)

If after 1 hour your account is still not activated:
- Contact **support@mammouth.ai**
- Attach your invoice
- Mention that you followed this guide

## 🔄 Case 3 — You subscribed earlier than today and you can see your past conversations

**Symptoms:**
- You can see your past conversations
- The platform asks you to resubscribe

**What's happening:**
Your renewal payment did not succeed.

**What to do:**
- Simply take a new subscription
- Your new subscription will start today

## 🔒 Case 4 — You lost access to your Google account

If Google is blocking your login, we unfortunately cannot resolve this.

**What to do:**
- Contact Google support and follow their recovery process
- This may take a few days

**If you cannot recover your Google account:**
- Make sure to cancel your subscription from your bank to avoid renewal

**If you cannot cancel from your bank:**
Contact: **support@mammouth.ai**
Provide:
- First name and last name
- Last invoice (or amount + date of last payment)
- Last 4 digits of your credit card
- Email address of the lost account

We will cancel the renewal for you.
