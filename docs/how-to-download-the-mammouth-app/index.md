# Install the app

Mammouth lets you use AI anytime, on all your devices.

## How to download the Mammouth app?

::: info ➡️ The Mammouth app is available directly on [mammouth.ai](http://mammouth.ai), from your browser.
:::

::: info ⚠️ The Mammouth app is not available on the Apple Store. This guide explains how to install it otherwise on all your devices.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>In Mozilla Firefox</strong> (Windows only)</summary>

1. Go to https://mammouth.ai/app/a/default
2. In the browser search bar, **click on the "Install Mammouth" icon**

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/install_firefox.png" alt="download mammouth desktop app" style="max-height: 850px" >

::: info 💡 Firefox only supports the Mammouth app on Windows.
If you're using another operating system, you'll need to open another browser to install Mammouth.
:::

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>In Safari</strong></summary>

1. Go to https://mammouth.ai/app/a/default
2. Click on the share icon
3. Select "Add to Dock" and confirm

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/install_safari.png" alt="download mammouth desktop app on Safari" style="max-height: 500px" >

::: info 💡 _Even though Safari uses the same installation flow as for shortcuts, you're actually installing the same app as on other browsers._

## 📱 On iPhone or Android

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/iOS_and_android.png" alt="iOS and Android" style="height: 35px" />

<br>

<details>
<summary><strong>On iPhone</strong></summary>
  
From Safari or Chrome:
1. Go to https://mammouth.ai/app/a/default
2. Click on the share icon
3. Select "Add to Home Screen" and confirm

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/chrome_OS.png" alt="iOS download instructions">

::: info 💡 Even though iOS uses the same installation flow as for shortcuts, you're actually installing the same app as on other devices.
:::

</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>On Android</strong></summary>

**From the Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ai.mammouth.application**

**From Google Chrome**:
1. Go to https://mammouth.ai/app/a/default
2. Open the sidebar menu from the top left
3. Click on the settings button at the bottom left
4. Select "Install app"

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/download_from_sidebar.png" alt="add app on android" style="max-width: 100%; max-height: 400px">

<details>
<summary>You don't see the <i>Install app</i> button or are using Mozilla Firefox?</summary>

There's another method:

1. Open your browser settings
2. Select "Add to Home screen" and confirm the installation

<img src="/docs/how-to-download-the-mammouth-app/tuto_android_EN.png" alt="add on android" style="max-height: 500px" >

</details>
</details>
