# For Teams and Businesses

## 👥 Roles and Permissions

Team and Organization accounts are built around three role levels:

| Role | Rights |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full access to all Team page features, including plan management, billing and permissions |
| **Admin** | Can invite, activate and deactivate members and other admins. Cannot modify the subscription, billing or permissions |
| **Member** | No access to the Team page |

### Bulk Invitations

Members can be invited **individually** or **in groups of up to 100 people** via a simple copy-paste of an email list (from a CSV file or a spreadsheet).

### User Privacy

Each user's conversations and documents are **strictly confidential** and remain their own property. No team member can access another user's conversations, except through explicit sharing via:
- conversation sharing,
- custom mammouth sharing.

## 🔒 Permissions

At their discretion, **Owners** can restrict certain features for all team members:

- ❌ Disable specific **AI providers**
- ❌ Disable **API** access
- ❌ Disable **video** generation

## 🦣 Shared Mammouths

A **Mammouth** (personalized AI workspace) can be shared within a team with two access levels:

- **Read-only**: invited members can use the Mammouth without modifying its instructions or associated files.
- **Read and edit**: invited members can modify the instructions and associated files.

> ⚠️ Sharing a Mammouth **does not imply** sharing the conversations that take place within it. Each conversation remains private by default.

### Conversation Sharing

Conversation sharing is possible, but must be **explicitly enabled** on the relevant conversation. Sharing is done via a **URL link**.

## 🔀 Multiplan Option

It is possible to **mix plans** (Starter, Standard and Expert) within the same team, allowing you to tailor access to each collaborator's actual needs.

## 💳 Payment Options and Billing

### Online Payment *(recommended)*

Available at [mammouth.ai/pricing](https://mammouth.ai/pricing), with the following payment methods:

- 💳 Credit/debit card
- 🅿️ PayPal
- 🛍️ Klarna
- 🏦 SEPA direct debit *(Europe only)*

These payment methods allow for **fully autonomous** subscription management.

**Billing:** An invoice is automatically sent by email upon each payment and is also available in your personal account area.

> 💡 For businesses: when registering on the payment page, select **"I'm purchasing as a business"** and enter your European VAT number.

---

### Bank Transfer or Administrative Order

Payment by bank transfer is accepted under the following conditions:

- **Annual commitment required**
- **No plan mixing**: the entire team must be on the same plan

**For any quote request**, write to [contact@mammouth.ai](mailto:contact@mammouth.ai) with the following information:
- Company name, address, and registration number
- Desired plan
- Number of licenses

> ⏱️ Each license addition requires a written email request and will be processed **within 72 business hours**.
