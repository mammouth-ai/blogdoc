### Major Components

- **Characters:** gender, age, emotion, position
- **Objects:** distinctive accessories, decorative elements
- **Scene:** precise setting (jungle, cozy living room, Parisian street)
- **Movement:** still or in action?

### **Visual Styles and Inspirations**

Photography

- **Realistic**: detailed photography, high definition
- **Black and White**: pronounced contrast, dramatic lighting

Classical Paintings

- **Watercolor**: fluid colors, transparency
- **Impressionism**: fragmented brushstrokes, light capture (Monet)
- **Van Gogh**: swirling whorls, generous impasto
- **Surrealism**: dreamlike, distorted reality, unbridled imagination (Dal√≠)
- **Cubism**: spatial deconstruction, multiple perspectives (Picasso)

Decorative Arts

- **Gothic**: mysterious darkness, monumental architecture
- **Baroque**: sumptuous ornaments, gilding, theatricality
- **Art Nouveau**: organic curves, Mucha floral motifs
- **Art Deco**: stylized geometry, 1920s elegance

Graphic Arts

- **Minimalist**: refined composition, elegant sobriety
- **Pop Art**: saturated colors, comic book codes, Warhol style
- **Street Art**: urban expression, spray paint, Banksy style

Animation

- **Anime/Manga**: expressive lines, vivid colors, Japanese style
- **Studio Ghibli**: visual poetry, soft light, dreamlike atmosphere
- **Pixel Art**: retro-gaming aesthetic, 8-bit rendering

Imaginary Worlds

- **Cyberpunk**: futuristic, bright neons, technological dystopia
- **Steampunk**: Victorian-tech, copper gears, steam era
- **Fantasy**: imaginary universes, legendary creatures

### **Lighting and Effects**

- **Lighting:** natural, dramatic, cinematic
- **Warm tones**: red/orange/yellow (energy)
- **Cool tones**: blue/green/purple (calm)
- **Pastel**: muted softness (lightness)
- **Effects and textures**: contrast, saturation, grain, artistic blur

### Angles and Perspective

- **Close-up**: detailed facial expressions
- **Aerial view**: panorama, overall staging
- **Fish-eye**: immersive effect, dynamic distortion
- **Wide shot**: extended scene, ideal for landscapes/environments
- **Low angle**: grandeur, power, view from below
- **Backlight**: silhouettes, cinematic effect

### Image Format

- **Orientation**: portrait, landscape
- **Ratio: `--ar 1:1` (square)**, **`--ar 2:3`**, **`--ar 3:2`**, **`--ar 4:5`**, **`--ar 5:4`**, **`--ar 4:3`**, **`--ar 3:4`**, **`--ar 16:9`** or **`--ar 9:16`**

    <span class="icon">‚ö†</span>
<div class="content"><strong>Pitfalls to Avoid:</strong>
    <ul>
        <li>
                <strong>Information overload</strong>: too much information overwhelms the AI and generates incoherent images
        </li>
        <li>
            <strong>Lack of precision</strong>: a vague prompt produces unpredictable and random results
        </li>
        <li>
            <strong>Contradictory instructions</strong>: the AI cannot resolve conflicting directives
        </li>
        <li>
            <strong>Poor prioritization</strong>: always place essential elements at the beginning of the prompt
        </li>
    </ul>
</div>
</div>
